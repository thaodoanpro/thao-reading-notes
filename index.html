Thao's Reading Notes

Elon Musk

By Walter Isaacson

A book that can help you understan Elon Musk's major life and career events that shaped the person he is today. It describes the challenges Musk encountered and how he faced them.

Hillbilly Elegy

By JD Vance

This candid life story of JD Vance helps readers understand the social and economical challenges faced by many white working class people in America, and how one overcame these challenges with help of family, grit, and favorable turn of fate.

Microbiome Thyroid: Restore Your Gut and Heal Your Hidden Thyroid Disease

By Raphael Kellman, MD

This book explains the role of thyroid to our health and the importance of microbiome to maintain an optimal thyroid.
